Trial Period

Important points to keep in mind during the trial period:

- You can continue your existing work full time. So there is no risk.

- Typically candidates invest two hours a day. And two months of trial period can make the decision easier for both the parties.

- The goal of the trial period is to provide free (and unpaid) training to the candidate while closely observing their learning abilities and performance.

- Technical skills are just one piece of the puzzle. There are many other aspects that decide whether a company and the candidate can work together for a long term.

- You are not full-time with the company yet. And the decision depends on your performance.

The things that matter to Freshbits are:

- Willingness to give 100% onto the task at hand.

- Self-learning attitude to solve newer problems.

- Attention to details while discussing new requirements.

- Timely delivery.

- Readable and maintainable code.

- Supporting the projects for lifetime.

- No fear of making mistakes. And seriousness not to repeat them.
