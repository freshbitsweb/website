Referral Commission Policy

Anyone can refer candidates and earn referral commission.

The role of the referrer is to request the candidate to share their resume directly with the company and provide the referrer's name in the same email. Referrers are not involved in the hiring process beyond this initial step. Interviews and other formalities will happen as per the normal process of recruitment only.

If someone refers any candidates and the company decides to hire that candidate then whoever referred the candidates will be rewarded with 20% of a 1-month of candidate's salary for the new hire. 5% TDS from the total amount will be deducted from each payment.

50% of the referral amount will be paid on completion of 3 months from the date of joining the candidate and the remaining on the completion of another 3 months. The amount will be processed with the completed month's payroll.

When referring a candidate for a position at our company, please ask the candidate to send their resume directly to us. Also tell them to specify the your (referrer's) name.

You would not be eligible for any referral amount in case the candidate's resume already exists in the company's databank.

The selection of referred candidates depends on the interview. Making a referral does not mean the final selection.

Only the candidate who meets the qualification required for a given position will be eligible for referral. The best referral program nowhere justifies any biased recruitment process.

There is no limit to the number of referrals made by you.

The company reserves the right to revise, or modify any or all clauses of this policy depending upon the demand of the business.
